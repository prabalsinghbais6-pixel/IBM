About Josh AI

Josh is a personal wellness AI coach built on IBM watsonx Orchestrate. It combines powerful language understanding with deep health and fitness knowledge, giving you a single conversational companion for a healthier, more balanced life.

What Josh can help you with

  • Building a personalised daily routine around your goals and schedule
  • Creating balanced meal plans, macro breakdowns, and healthy recipes
  • Teaching mindfulness, breathing exercises, and stress-relief techniques
  • Motivating you with coaching, goal-setting, and accountability check-ins
  • Providing workout plans, exercise tips, and injury recovery guidance
  • Answering any question about nutrition, sleep, fitness science, or well-being

How to use Josh

Click Open Chat in the top-right corner, or navigate to the Chat page from the home screen. Type your question or request in plain English — no special syntax required.

Josh maintains context within a session, so you can follow up naturally: "What about last quarter?" — and Josh will understand.

Tips for best results

  • Be specific — the more context you give, the better the answer
  • You can ask follow-up questions to refine a response
  • Ask Josh to "explain step by step" for complex topics
  • Use phrases like "summarise", "list", or "compare" to shape the output
